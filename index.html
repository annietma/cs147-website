Cherry

Hi, we're Cherry!

We want to help you cherish your long-distance relationships.

  • Learn More

As people transition into new life stages, relationships with friends, family, and partners can shift to long-distance relationships. LDRs often grow weak as people become busy with their individual lives. Although forms of staying connected already exist, people often struggle and hesitate to initiate meaningful conversations online due to a lack of mindfulness, time, and comfort in asking deeper, personal questions. Cherry is a mobile application designed to facilitate the inquiry of deep questions between users. Cherry aims to prove that staying close isn’t just about proximity.

Features:

Check In With Daily Generated Questions

Daily pre-generated questions based on user-preferred topics help users move past the entry barrier of initiating a conversation. These questions are only asked and answered once a day, requiring a low time commitment from users. Pre-generated questions remove user hesitancy to appear too intrusive, while still allowing for personal—even taboo—topics to be explored.

Get Deep With Your Friends Through Rapid Fire!

Cherry's rapid-fire sessions let you go back and forth in asking deep, intimate questions without just being restricted to one conversation starter. Get deep into topics, connect over extended periods, and really get to know one another in one extended, intimate period. Not only does Cherry provide you with our own conversations starters, but we let you to type in your own questions to let you take control of the conversation.

Let Your Creative Side Go Wild!

From basic text responses to flexible voice recordings to free-hand drawings, Cherry lets you tune in with your creativity side when engaging with your close relationships. Communication shouldn't be limited by just one medium. Whatever you want to do, Cherry is here for you.
